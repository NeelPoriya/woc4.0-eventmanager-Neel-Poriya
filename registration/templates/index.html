About Us

Creating and managing contests can be quite time consuming and hectic. No worries, we at Event Manager have come for the rescue. Now, you can focus on other things, and we will take care of making for your event and enable participantion in your event.

Fast & Easy


With our service, you can create new events and let other people participate in no time. Each user who enters the site, will be able to choose one of the upcoming contests and after registration, user will get a notification at their provided mobile number. They will be provided with the event ID and password which is unique for every event.

Quick Remainders


Each participant will get all the event details through a message on their provided mobile number, using these they will be able to look at the dashboard and see which event they are part of. Also, we will provide a remainder message before an hour of beginning of the event.

Trust worthy


The data you provide will only be visible to the admin, else no one can have access to the information you provide. So, your privacy is 100% protected. We assure you that your name and email will be the only thing visible to the event organisers as well. For more information about privary security, feel free to contact us.
